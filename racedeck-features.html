For Athletes

Your race history, personal bests and training — all in one place.

Personal Best Tracking

Automatic PB recognition across every RaceDeck event you enter. Self-reported times shown with a transparent unverified indicator; platform-recorded results get a verified badge.

Results Dashboard

Every result from every event — filterable by sport, distance, stroke, season. See trends at a glance and track your progression over time.

Wearable Integration

Connect Garmin, Apple Health, Whoop, Polar, Suunto, COROS, Wahoo and Zwift via Open Wearables. Heart rate, cadence and pace overlay on your race results.

Shareable Profile

A public URL that works like a digital race CV — share it on social media, send it to scouts, or embed it in your club bio. SSO login with Google, Apple or Facebook.

Training Plans

Follow structured training plans built by your coach — synced directly to your watch. Covers swim, bike and run with TrainingPeaks-grade metrics collection.

Privacy by Default

Four-tier data visibility model. You control exactly who sees what — from anonymous aggregate data to full profile access. GDPR, CCPA and UAE PDPL compliant.

For Schools

Run sports days, manage students and share results with parents — safely.

Bulk Athlete Management

Upload entire year groups via CSV. Organise students by year, class, form tutor or house. Bulk edits, bulk entries and bulk download — no individual data entry needed.

Parent Invitations & Consent

Mandatory parent email on all student profiles. Automated invitation flow with consent capture. Parents see only their child's results — never anyone else's.

Invitation-Only Events

Host inter-school galas by sending secure invitations to other schools. 30-day expiry with re-send controls. Each school manages its own entries independently.

Participation Certificates

Auto-generated printable PDF certificates with a verification QR code. Host school logo displayed prominently. Participating school names in text.

Team Scoring & Houses

Configurable points tables for house competitions. Auto-calculated standings across events. Perfect for inter-house swimming galas and athletics days.

Age-Based Data Protection

Region-configurable age thresholds for profile ownership transitions. School-specific data stays with the school when a student moves on. Core profile belongs to the athlete.

For Clubs & Coaches

Performance tools, squad management and season-long competition tracking.

Coach Dashboard

See every athlete grouped by training squad, age group or discipline. Top-N leaderboards, scouting filters by PB range and improvement trend, and side-by-side comparison views.

Training Plans & Metrics

Build periodised training plans and push structured workouts direct to athletes' watches. Full TrainingPeaks-grade metrics: TSS, CTL, ATL, power zones, pace targets and more.

Leagues & Event Series

Run season-long competitions with configurable points. Monthly series, cross-club leagues and tournament brackets — all with automatic standings and progression.

Coach Certifications

Coaching qualifications stored once on the coach profile and auto-populated to every affiliated club. Expiry dates surface automatically — no manual tracking needed.

Organisation Hierarchy

Parent-child structure for multi-team clubs. "4th Lane Sports" contains "4th Lane Swim Team" which contains "4th Lane Swim Team Dubai". Federation affiliations via many-to-many links.

CRM & Athlete Retention

Segmented communications, retention analytics and loyalty tracking. Know which athletes are slipping away before they leave.

For Event Organisers

From registration to live results — run professional events at any scale.

Registration & Payments

Stripe-powered with multi-currency support (GBP, EUR, USD, AED). Athletes pay 5% + flat fee by default, or organisers can absorb the cost. Embeddable forms for your own website.

QR Check-In & Bib Assignment

Scan, search by name, assign bibs and capture waivers — all from one screen. Chip assignment for hardware timing. Real-time check-in count for event-day awareness.

Hardware Timing Integration

Connect RACE RESULT, MYLAPS, CTS and Omega timing systems with zero-coding onboarding. QR/NFC-based device setup. Save your device library per organisation for reuse across events.

Live Results & Broadcast

Configurable live results table — you choose the columns, viewers customise their view. Venue scoreboards, streaming overlays and real-time updates via the native app.

File Format Support

Full read/write for Lenex 3.0 and SDIF — the open standards. Read-only import for Hy-Tek HY3 and CL2, enabling painless migration from legacy systems. Plus CSV export.

Heats, Finals & Progression

Automatic seeding from heats to semi-finals to finals. Configurable advancement rules. Team scoring, DQ management with full audit trail, and official/referee interfaces.

Multi-User Org Roles

Delegate registration staff, hardware operators, event officials and meet directors — all with role-based access. Officials register the night before with persistent, account-linked sessions.

White-Label & Custom Branding

Premium tiers unlock full event branding — custom DNS via A/CNAME records, platform logo removal on certificates, and fully generated custom event websites.

Built for the real world

One platform. Every sport. Every scale — from a school swimming gala to a World Triathlon Series qualifier.
